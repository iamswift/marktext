# A Weekend Guide to Kyoto

Kyoto is easily reached by train from Osaka, and the trip takes less than an hour on the regular rapid service.

Use the `day pass` if you plan on visiting more than three temples, since individual tickets add up quickly.

Arrive early to avoid the crowds of tour groups that gather at the main gate after nine in the morning.

Many travelers also recommend the `bamboo grove` early in the morning before the crowds arrive.

If it starts raining, most temple grounds still allow visitors to walk the covered corridors.

A short packing list for the trip:

- Comfortable shoes for walking on gravel paths.
- A light jacket, since evenings can be cooler than the daytime.
- A refillable water bottle for the summer months.

Whatever route you take, plan for at least one full day at a relaxed pace so you enjoy the trip rather than rushing between sights.
