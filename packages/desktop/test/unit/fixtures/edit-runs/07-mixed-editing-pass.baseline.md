# A Weekend Guide to Kyoto

Kyoto is easily reached by train from Osaka, and the trip takes less then an hour on the regular rapid service.

Utilize the **day pass** if you plan on visiting more then three temples, since individual tickets add up quickly.

Arrive early in order to avoid the crowds of tour groups that gather at the main gate after nine in the morning.

Many travelers additionally recommend the *bamboo grove* early in the morning before the crowds arrive.

If it starts raning, most temple grounds still allow visitors to walk the covered corridors.

A short packing list for the trip:

- Comfortable shoes for walking on gravel paths.
- A light jacket, since evenings can be cooler then the daytime.
- A refillable water bottle for the summer months.

Whatever route you take, plan for at least one full day at a relaxed pace so you actually enjoy the trip instead of rushing between sights.
